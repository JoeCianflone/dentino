Blueprint Text Reminder FAQ

1.What carriers support text services?

The following carriers support Text Services:
  • AT&T
  • Alltel
  • Boost
  • CBW
  • Nextel
  • nTelos
  • Sprint
  • T-Mobile®
  • U.S. Cellular®
  • Verizon Wireless
  • Virgin Mobile

2.How do I cancel text reminder services for the Full Pay Reminders program?

From your cell phone, please send STOP to 37029.

3.Can I register more than one eligible Chase Slate credit card on my Cell phone number?

Yes, you can register more than one eligible Chase Slate account.

4.How much does it cost for me to participate?

There is no cost through Chase. However, there may be charges from your carrier depending upon your cell phone plan.

5.Can I call Chase to register in the text reminder service for the Full Pay Reminders program?

No. It is necessary for you to register through the website.

6.I registered for text reminder services. Why am I not receiving any messages?

You should have received a confirmation message after registering through the website. If you didn't receive a confirmation, it is possible that your carrier does not support the Text Services or you provided an incorrect phone number.

7.How many messages can I expect to receive through the Full Pay Reminders program?

You can expect to receive up to 1 message per account each week after completing registration of your account.

8.If I register multiple accounts on my cell phone will I receive multiple reminders each month?

After you register and confirm the registration for each account.

9.What types of purchases are included in the Full Pay Reminders program?

Gas, grocery, or dining purchases may result in a Full Pay reminder being sent to your mobile phone.

10.How long will I receive Full Pay Reminders?

You will receive Full Pay Reminders until March 15, 2012, or until you activate Full Pay on your account, or you opt out of receiving reminders.

11.Why do I need to provide last name, last 4 digits of account number, and zip code to enroll in the program?

We need to be able to identify your account in order to enroll you in the text reminder program.
