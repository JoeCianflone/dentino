You will earn a $10 gift card of your choice if one or more new Automatic Bill Payments are made at a service provider that accepts MasterCard for payment between October 15 – November 15, 2012. A new payment is defined as an Automatic Bill Payment at a service provider using the Miles & More MasterCard card issued to the primary cardholder or any authorized user(s) when a similar transaction with the same service provider has not been posted to the account within the 60 days prior to October 15, 2012. To qualify new Automatic Bill Payments must: (i) be completed using a valid 16-digit Miles & More MasterCard card number by November 15, 2012, (ii) be identified to MasterCard by the service provider as a recurring payment, and (iii) post to the Miles & More MasterCard card account by December 31, 2012. Please allow 8 to 10 weeks after the end of the promotion to receive your gift card. In order to receive your gift card, your account must remain open and in good standing at the time the gift card is fulfilled. The gift card will be awarded to the primary account holder (as determined by the MasterCard account records) named on the account accessed by the MasterCard card used to make the qualifying purchases. Purchases from the same account but different cards cannot be combined.
